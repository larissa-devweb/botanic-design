Botany, also called plant science(s), plant biology or phytology, is the science of plant life and a branch of biology. A botanis, plant scientist or phytologist is a scientis who specialises in this field.

It originated in prehistory as herbolism with the efforts of early humans to identify - and later cultivate - edible, medicinal and poisonous plants, making it one of the oldest branches of science.
